How to Play

Welcome to the City Simulation Game! Your goal is to manage a city by adjusting taxes, building infrastructure, and balancing resources. Keep an eye on your population, income, and power supply!

  • Use the tax slider to adjust the tax rate.
  • Build schools, hospitals, houses, and renewable energy plants to grow your city.
  • Monitor your population, income, and power demand.
  • Watch out for natural disasters that can impact your city.
